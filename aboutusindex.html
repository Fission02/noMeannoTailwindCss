Our Story

At NO Means NO!, we believe in the power of awareness, compassion, and action. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful realization: that sexual assault is a pervasive issue affecting countless lives. We recognized the urgent need to make a change, to break the silence, and to offer support to survivors. Founded in 2022-23, our organization has been committed to creating a safer and more empathetic world. We are a community of advocates, survivors, allies, and supporters who share a common goal: to end the stigma surrounding sexual assault and provide a safe space for survivors to heal and thrive. Over the years, we have worked tirelessly to raise awareness, educate, and empower individuals to prevent sexual assault. We've also provided resources, counseling, and a network of understanding for those affected by it. Our journey is ongoing, and we invite you to join us in our mission to create a world free from sexual violence. Together, we can make a difference.
